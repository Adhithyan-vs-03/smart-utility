About Smart Public Utility

Smart Public Utility Management System is a modern digital platform designed to streamline public utility services such as electricity, water, and gas billing through automation and secure web technologies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to digitize public utility management, reduce manual errors, and improve transparency between administrators, staff, and consumers.

The system enables automated billing, real-time tracking, secure payments, and centralized data management to ensure efficiency and reliability.

Automated Billing

Generate accurate bills instantly based on meter readings.

Online Payments

Secure digital payment integration for fast transactions.

Analytics Dashboard

Monitor revenue, unpaid bills, and system performance.

Secure Access

Role-based authentication for Admin, Staff, and Consumers.
